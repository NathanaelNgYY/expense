B-lite: filter and pin

If the bottom sheet feels too expensive for this release, tapping a date can filter the existing ledger and pin the add action directly above it.

Cheaper: reuses the current date filters and ledger.
Less direct: the calendar, add action, and entries compete in one scroll surface.
Good fallback: the information model stays compatible with a future sheet.
